THE ACADEMY

The Alpha academy in England is located in the town of stammford,in the centre of the UK.The academy has 10 years of experience coaching football to young talented players.

HISTORY

The academy was founded by the malinger brothers together with graham stamer,who began their involvement with football in britain in the years 2000-10 as agents acting between professional footballer and teams.They also organised events to bring together talents scouts with footballers from all parts of british isles with a view to signing them. After 7 years collaborating with major teams from the Premier League, they also decided to create their own academy which was innitially based in Nene park,irthlinborough,in the country of Northamptonshire.


After a number of years without a permanent location, in 2015 the academy moved to its permanent home of Stamford, where it remains and where the academy benefits from excellent sports facilities from which they deliver high quality coaching. The friendly atmosphere of the town means student feel at home, enjoying living in a residence together with other young people and which is situated very close to training grounds.

Who?

The Alpha Football Academy seeks young and talented footballers around the globe with the sole purpose of helping them achieve their dreams by helping them kickstart their career in the best way possible.

When?

The Alpha Football Academy offers admission to qualified candidates after they must have passed the various screening test exercices. Admission is offered twice a year through the regular academy programme.

How?

By leveraging our state of the art facilities and world class management staff, Participants are immersed in professional football participation, building their skill as well as their confidence to help them integrate seamlessly into professional careers at the end of the programme.

STUDENT LIFE AT THE ACADEMY

Alpha football Academy ensures safety is essential. As a result, we employ a full-time qualified physiotherapist to help with footall related injuries. Furthermore, private medical cover is offered to all players which ensures immediate attention to any serious injuries picked up at the academy. Please note this medical cover does not cover for any injuries picked up before joining the academy.
Alpha Football Academy also provides a Welfare Officer and all the residential staff hold the relevant DBS checks and child protection certificates. All students on the full-time programme are registered with a local GP and dentist whilst also remaining registered with their doctor back home for holiday periods


STUDENT LIFE

Excursions

During your time at the Academy, some optional excursions are arranged. In recent seasons, these have included trips to watch live football matches, cinema trips, swimming, paintballing, go-karting, team building, shopping, gyms, ten-pin bowling, Alton Towers and many other forms of entertainment. All these excursions are fully supervised by Alpha Academy staffs.

Weekends

Alpha Football Academy is set-up for players to stay for secen days of the week however if they wish, players are allowed to leave the Academy at weekends with parental consent providing they return by sunday evening.

Meals

Alpha Football Academy caters for three maels a day, seven days a week during the academic year. Over the years we have had many international students catering for different cuisines and any dietry requirements.

Religions

Many international students attend coming from a wide variety of cultures. The Academy will always try to cater for religious requirements when possible.
