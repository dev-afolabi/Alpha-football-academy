THE AlPHA ACADEMY PRO SOCCER TRY-OUTS

Get scouted by the network that represents Football Scouts Worldwide

Want to be scouted by the network that represent Football Scouts Worldwide at the UK's most succesful football trials? Then good news you're in the right place!

The Alpha Football Academy in conjuction with the Professional Football Scouts Association deliver the UK's most succesful football trials events in and around the UK, attended by our members across the country. we deliver football trials in various locations with our home base at UClan's world-class facilities in Preston. The Alpha Academy PRO Soccer Try-Outs will be suppoorted by Adidas and will have scouts attending connected with all clubs within the Premier League, Championship, League One and Two, La-liga and The Bundesliga.

The Alpha Football Academy has decided to collaborate with The Professional Football Scouts Association who represents Football Scouts Worldwide and with a member base sitting within it's thousand, we have decided to best utilise this network through Soccer Try-Outs, giving the younger generation the best opportunity at finding a professional or semi-professional footbal club. Please note places are extremely limited so if you wish to attend then please book early to avoid missing out on the UK's most succesful football trials.

WHEN AND WHERE

The Alpha Academy hold trial days in and around the country with our primary base at UCLan's World Class facilities at their Sports Arena in Preston. UCLan has internationally recognised facilities and is one of the Worlds leading departments in Sports Science which is perfect as the Alpha Academy aim to add further metrics to our trials days by partnering up with VXSports and providing sporting diagnostics like the professionals!

WHO WILL ATTEND ON THE DAY

The more appropriate question to ask is who does not attend the Alpha Academy Pro Soccer Try-Outs. we have a network of thousands of football scouts as members of the Professional Football Scouts Association. We will have our co-founders and senior scouts at leading football clubs attending. All players highlighted on the day will then be sent to our network.

WHAT HAPPENS ON THE DAY

The Alpha Academy Trials are a full day event 9am to 4pm. The AM consists of all ages coaching then as we move into the afternoon, we start with the trial matches. Snacks and refreshments are availabile on the day to rehydrate and fill up on that much needed energy boost.

VIDEO ANALYSIS

The Alpha Academy Pro Soccer Try-Outs will be using top of the range video analysis software to give our trialist the option to record their trials day experience. Having your skills on video gives you the edge over the competition and is vital in standing out from the crowd. One of the main reasons the Alpha Academy decided to launch the video analysis option was to enable us send the recorded videos of impressive trialist to scouts and hiring managers not present on trial days. Even though a football CV is useful information in guaging the level at which you have played at, it was always difficult to validate the skills on paper.

HOW TO BOOK

Places for the Alpha Academy Pro Soccer Try-Outs will be extremely limited, so please make sure you book early to avoid disappointment. if you would like to book onto the football trials day, please follow the link below and fill in all the information required. Once you have booked onto the football trials you will be sent a full itinerary within 4 weks prior to the start date, detailing the whole days action, what time to arrive and what to expect.
