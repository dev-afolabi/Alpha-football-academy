FOOTBALL FACILITIES

Alpha Football Academy are based at the newly built £5.5m development at the Borderville Sports Complex in Stamford.
The Academy matches will be held at the capacity stadium, with training held on the floodlit full-sixe 3G all-weather pitch and grass training pitches. The Centre also boasts a sports hall, a fully equipped gymnasium, dressing rooms, medical room, canteen and classsrooms. These facilities have enabled the Academy to increase its intake of students each year to keep up with the increasing high demand.


FURTHER INFORMATION

stadiums

Each year, the Academy holds its final year Showcase Match and Awards Presentation at some of the top stadiums in the UK. In recent years we have traveled to The Hawthorns, home of West Bromich Albion, the Ricoh Arena, home of Coventry City and most recently The King Power Stadium, home of the former Premier League champions Leceister City.

St George's Park - Burton

Alpha Football Academy has held so many events and training sessions at St Georges Park, the FA's National Football Centre. The Centre is the training base for the 24 England National Teams, and the facilities include a multipurpose indoor sports hall, 11 full size outdoor pitches, one elite DESSO training pitch which is an exact replica of the pitch at Wembley, and two hotels.

Oakham School

Some training sessions and matches are also held at Oakham School, one of the leading private schools in the area boasting outstanding sports facilities. With 40 acres of the best grass playing areas in the country, a multi-purpose sports centre, an indoor pool, two floodlit all-weather pitches, a stregth and conditioning room and a fitness centre, the school has become a well-recognised training for national squads.
