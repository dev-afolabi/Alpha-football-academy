BLENDED LEARNING SPORTS DEGREE

In association with the world renowned centre for sporting excellence, Loughborough College, the Alpha Football Academy offers the opportunity to study either a BA(Hons) Sport Management or BSc Sport Coaching degree via blended learning.
Blended learning provides the perfect combination of online/distance learnibg and contact day attendance, enabling students to perfectly balance their studies alongside the academy's football development programme.
Alpha Football Academy can now offer the degree courses to full-time students who start with us in January of each year, as well as September. The degrees are both validated by Nottingham Trent University.

COURSE INFORMATION

With renowned provision for high quality teaching, your learning will include online lectures, seminars and activities, as well as independent study.
Assessment include a range of essays, projects, presentation and reports. Such diversity will allow you to experience various modes of academic and professional practise, developing your abilities and refining your skills in preparation for your next steps.
With an excellent support system in place, academic tutors will work closely with you throughout the duration of your programme, supporting your development and tracking your academic progress, to ensure that you achieve your personal best.

BA (Hons) Sports Management (Blended Learning)

The programme provides opportunities to develop your management skills. You will cultivate a knowledge and understanding of operations, finance, human resources, leadership qualities, which will be applied to sports facilities and events.
You will put your theory into practice through applied studies and assessments. The Sports specific modules include Sports Development and Physical Activity, Lifestyle, and Wellbeing.

BSc Sport Coaching (Blended Learning)

The programme provides opportunities to develop your teaching and coaching skills. You will cultivate a knowledge of the disciplines underpinning effective sports coaching and performance for different ages and abilities.
You will put your theory into practice through applied studies, placements and assessments.
