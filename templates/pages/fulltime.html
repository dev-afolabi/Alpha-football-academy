FULL-TIME FOOTBALL PROGRAMME

Enrolment onto our full-time programme commences in september and january of every year. if the student is studying one of our academic qualifications, the course duration is one to three years, dependent on the qualification being taken.

The academy's intensive training programme has been designed to enhance every individuals game. Our training sessions and matches are led by highly qualified UEFA A coaches, including experienced former professional players and managers.

Sessions focus on improving individual technique, as well as developing tactical awareness and an understanding of the game. These sessions, alongside strenghtening physical attributes through a robust conditioning programme delivered by Chronos Gym, leaves the student fully prepared for the modern game.

COURSE INFORMATION

Academic football team

The academy football team, Alpha stamford, are an FA Charter Standard Club, competing in the National League U19 youth aliance, British Colleges U19 League and Midland Football U21 League, as well as local county FA tournaments.

Friendly fixtures are regularly arranged against Premier League and Football League oppositions, whilst specific showcase matches are staged in front of Premier League and English Football League scouts, giving each student a chance to test and benchmark their development against professional clubs in England.

Each year, subject to EU funding, students in their second year can attend a two-week placement with a professional club in Europe. Placements in the past have taken place in Belgium and Spain, enabling students to receive further training specific to European Football, whilst enhancing opportunities of progression within the game.

Football activities

Away from the pitch itself, a number of activities are arranged to further enhance the students football development. Examples of some of these can include:

  • Guest speaker sessions with former professional footballers and managers.
  • Sessions about contracts with FIFA-approved Player Agents.
  • Expert coaching sessions from UEFA-qualified coaches.
  • Specialist goalkeeping sessions from UEFA-qualified coaches.
  • Specific strength and conditioning sessions with qualified instructors including yoga.
  • Fitness monitoring and testing
  • Physiotherapy sessions and first aid training.
  • Team building activities
  • Support with exit pathways and provisions of references.
