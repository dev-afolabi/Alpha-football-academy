EDUCATION AT THE ACADEMY

The Academy combines full-time football coaching with a wide range of education courses. We are proud of the diversity of our education programme and can offer full-time and part-time education to suit your needs. We have partnered with some UK's leading specialist education partners to offer class teaching and qualifications.

EDUCATION COURSES

Not sure which course to choose? Browse through our course guide below to give you an insight into all the education opportunities at the Alpha Football Academy.

BTEC Level 3 Sports Diploma

BTEC Level 3 Extended Diploma in Sport (Performance and Excellence).

English Language Programme

English Language Football Programme for players aged 16 and over from around the world.

A Level Business Studies

The Business Studies syllabus enables learners to understand and appreciate the nature and scope of business, and the role it plays in society.

Blended Learning Sports Degree

The Alpha Football Academy offers the opportunity to study either a BA(Hons) Sports Management or BSc Sport Coaching degree.
